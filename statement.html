Statement of Master Priyansh Patel~

Hello my name is Priyansh Patel.

I'm 19 years of age and I'm living with "Type 1 Diabetes" since the past 13 years. Being a type 1 diabetic doesn't mean I can't do anything. I can do anything and everything with proper diabetes management. Keeping a regular eye on my sugar levels and insulin doses, I'm able to design and perform my workouts efficiently as well. I follow a PPL( Push Pull Legs) workout routine. The main reasons behind me choosing this workout routine only is because I often face highs and lows out of nowhere, during or around my workout session. As a result, sometimes I'm not able to complete or hit my desired workout. But after following this workout pattern, even if I hit gym thrice a week, I'm able to train all my body parts. So, in a nutshell, keeping my blood sugar levels in range, managing my insulin doses and following this particular workout routine, I'm able train my full body in, without any highs and lows or any other complications.
